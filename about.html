About SHOPY

Welcome to SHOPY! We are building a vibrant online community in Trinidad and Tobago, dedicated to making buying goods easy, secure, and enjoyable for everyone. Our vision is to create a thriving digital marketplace dedicated to buyers across the nation. We aim to provide shoppers with a diverse and exciting range of products, all accessible from the comfort of their homes.


History of SHOPY

SHOPY, was originally a physical store in Port-of-Spain, but due to our growing market we decided to create an online storefront.
